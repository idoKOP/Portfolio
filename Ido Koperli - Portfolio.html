01 - Profile

Engineer who sells. Operator who builds.

A short, honest read on how I think and where I plug in.

I started in mission-critical defense environments, supporting communication systems for Iron Dome and later designing network infrastructure inside Rafael's advanced defense projects. That foundation shaped how I work: calm under pressure, technical by default, and accountable.

From there, I moved into technical instruction, delivering 200+ hours of lectures and preparing 60+ students for Cisco certifications. Teaching forced me to turn complex systems into clear language - a skill I now use in sales, business development, product thinking and stakeholder communication.

What I do well: translate technical depth into business clarity. I'm wired for ownership, fast iteration, and conversations that end in a "yes."

Today, my fintech experience as a NOC Engineer adds live-production credibility: payment flows, incidents, dashboards, logs, KPIs, escalation, and cross-team communication across Product, Engineering, and Business. I don't define myself by the company name - I use the experience as proof that I can operate inside real business pressure.

On the side, I build real things - websites, lead systems, AI workflows, and a proud Co-Founder of ALIDON Automations for SMBs. I'm aiming for Product, Growth, BDR, SDR and BD roles where technical depth, business instinct, and fast execution actually matter.

/02
Logs → Uptime
Internal proposal · Rapyd · Ops × Onboarding
An internal initiative to rebuild Rapyd's NOC log taxonomy into a universal language layer - so a new analyst reads an incident the same way a 5-year veteran does.
Problem

Logs read fluently to R&D and DevOps - and like a foreign language to newer team members. Tribal knowledge bottlenecks every shift handover.

Solution

Standardize fields: source · destination · process type · error origin (Network / App / DB / 3rd-party / Internal Service) · short human description.

Business impact

Lower MTTR · lower MTTD · faster onboarding · less context switching · better SLA performance · less revenue lost per minute of incident.

My role

Author and pitch-owner. Framing the technical change as a revenue and people problem, not just an engineering one.

Movie Investment Intelligence Agent
AI · Data · Academic project
An AI agent that scores movie investment risk by fusing TMDB, IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes signals - including a critic-vs-audience gap model that flags productions critics love but audiences won't fund.
Hypothesis
